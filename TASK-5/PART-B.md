##### Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students in India



1. Use a unique, strong password for every account Combine letters, numbers \& symbols.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all key accounts. Enable it on Gmail, Instagram, WhatsApp, and banking apps
3. Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords — with anyone, ever.
4. Understand that receiving money does NOT need your UPI PIN (UPI Safety). Scammers send "collect" requests disguised as payments.
5. Verify the receiver's UPI ID carefully before every payment (UPI Safety)
6. Never buy from social media sellers without verifying them first.
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, email, or anything sensitive.
8. Tighten privacy settings on Instagram, Snapchat, and WhatsApp.
9. Be sceptical of "work from home" or "quick money" job offers online.
10. Check URLs carefully before entering credentials on any website
