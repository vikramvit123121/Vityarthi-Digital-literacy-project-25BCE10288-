## \## 5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for Responsible Social Media Use as a College Student



* ##### Do's



1\. Think before you post. Your digital footprint follows you long after college ends.



2\. Use privacy settings wisely. Regularly review who can see your content.



3\. Fact-check before sharing. Misinformation spreads fast.



4\. Build a positive personal brand. Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase projects, internships, and achievements. 



5\. Set boundaries around screen time. Use built-in app timers or scheduled "off" hours to protect your focus during study sessions and your sleep quality at night.



\---



* ##### Don'ts



1.Don't overshare personal information. Avoid posting your location in real time, your daily routine, or sensitive details.



2.Don't engage in online arguments or pile-ons. Disagree respectfully or disengage entirely.



3.Don't plagiarize or misrepresent content.



4.Don't let social media replace real connection. Prioritize in-person relationships and offline experiences.



5.Don't post under pressure or emotion. Draft it, sleep on it, then decide.
